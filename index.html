03 — About Kingdom Energy

A bridge between Chinese industrial capacity and global renewable markets.

Kingdom Energy was established in the United Kingdom in October 2019. Its main shareholders are United Renewables Limited, leading the management team, and Shanghai Electric Group — one of the world's largest manufacturers and EPC contractors in power generation equipment.

The company works closely with Shanghai Electric and other major Chinese partners to deliver investment and renewable energy services across Europe, the Middle East and beyond — allowing Chinese capital and industrial capacity to enter the global renewable energy market.
